说明：本题库由 6 篇写作题组成，期末试卷从中随机抽取 1 篇。15 分。

Test 1​

阅读下列招聘广告并以李明的名义写一封求职信。

Vacancy for waiter/waitress

  Buttercup Bakery is a busy restaurant offering great service, food and atmosphere. We offer great rates of pay and a professional, fun and safe working environment.

  We are looking for a qualified waiter or waitress to join our small professional team. The successful candidate will be responsible for serving food and drinks and cleaning tables. We offer a permanent position with a five-day week and weekend shifts. A minimum of two-year experience would be good, but not essential, yet a positive attitude, enthusiastic personality and winning smile are!

  Please apply to: The manager, Buttercup Bakery, 08 Bell Avenue, Yuba City，CA 95991.

参考答案：

Dear Sir or Madam,

  I saw your advertisement in today’s newspaper and I am writing to apply for the position of a qualified waiter. I have been interested in the restaurant business since I was a young boy. My mother is a manager of a restaurant in our town and has taught me a lot about serving food and drinks and cleaning tables.

  As you can see from the enclosed CV, I have a good school leaving certificate. I also have a good command of English, both spoken and written. I achieved very good marks in cooking.

  I spent my last two summer holidays working in a local snack bar, which I enjoyed very much.

  I would appreciate the chance to come for an interview and am available at any time.

  I look forward to your reply.

Yours faithfully,
Li Ming

Test 5​

说明：假定你是李俊，请根据下列内容填写请假申请表。

  • 姓名：李俊
  • 员工号：120435
  • 所在部门：市场部（Marketing Department）
  • 请假类别：病假（Sick Leave）
  • 拟请假日期：3 月 15 日-----24 日
  • 请假理由： 在过去数周，本人身体一直不适，由于本人在外地出差，无法及时就医。出差回来后，根据医生建议，需住院检查并治疗。请假时间为 10 天。

Leave Request Form

Employee Information

Name: Li Jun

Employee Number: 120435

Department: Marketing Department

Leave Type: Sick Leave

Starting Date: March 15th

Resumption Date: March 25th

Reasons for Leave：

This letter is to ask you for 10 days of sick leave. My doctor advised me to be treated in the hospital. Actually, I didn’t feel well in the past few weeks. But I didn’t see a doctor in time due to a business trip off the city. I apologize for any inconvenience caused by this leave and look forward to being able to return to the office as usual once I have sufficiently recovered.

Signature of Applicant: Li Jun

I apologize for any inconvenience caused by this leave and look forward to being able to return to the office as usual once I have sufficiently recovered.

Test 6​

请根据所给信息完成以下备忘录。

  • 日期：3 月 25 日
  • 发自：经理。 送达：全体员工。 主要内容：关于奖金
  • 内容： 去年公司取得很大业绩，每位员工下月将收到奖金 RMB3000 元，与下月工资一起发放。 希望大家继续努力工作，为公司的发展做出新的贡献。 祝愿公司明年取得更大的成绩。
  • 签名：Joan Blackburn

参考词汇：奖金：bonus, 业绩：achievement, 做贡献：make contributions

参考答案:

MEMO

Date: March 25th

From: Manager

To: All staff

Re: About bonus

Message:

I have the honor to inform you that our company has obtained great achievements since last year. It has been decided that each employee will receive a bonus of RMB3000 issued together with next month’s salary. I hope you can continue to work hard and make new contributions to the development of the company. Wish our company greater success next year.

Please feel free to contact me if you have any further proposals.

Signature: Joan Blackburn
