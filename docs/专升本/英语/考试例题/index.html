考试例题

一、单项选择题​

备注

共 10 小题；每小题 1 分，满分 10 分。

请从 A.B.C.D 四个选项中，选出可以填入空白处的最佳选项，并将答案写在答题卡上。

  1. The person playing the piano this time is ____ exchange student from France.

    • A. a
    • B. an
    • C. the
    • D. /

  2. lt’s usually not easy for people who are shy to ____ the fear to talk to strangers.

    • A. get over
    • B. get through
    • C. get on
    • D. get out

  3. The paper ____ used to be very popular, but now it is out of date.

    • A. tower
    • B. town
    • C. towel
    • D. tour

  4. The famous writer, ____ submit writings for China Daily is invited to give a speech in our university.

    • A. that
    • B. whose
    • C. whom
    • D. who

  5. By the time you arrive there this afternoon, the game ____ .

    • A. is to start
    • B. is starting
    • C. will start
    • D. will have started

  6. When you phoned me last night, I ____ with my friend on what's the plan for vacation.

    • A. argued
    • B. was arguing
    • C. has argued
    • D. argue

  7. ____ John ____ Jack may go with you because one of them must stay at home.

    • A. Either; or
    • B. Neither; nor
    • C. Both; and
    • D. Neither; or

  8. I strongly object ____ the proposals they had put forward at the meeting.

    • A. for
    • B. to
    • C. by
    • D. with

  9. I don't know ____ will make the final decision for our summer trip.

    • A. who
    • B. which
    • C. whom
    • D. what

  10. I am so worried about my brother. I am not sure ____ he has arrived.

    • A. which
    • B. what
    • C. whether
    • D. who

I have a neighbor. She looks young and beautiful, although she is over 70. She can do all the housework, read newspapers and learn something new on the Internet. I often see her do tai chi(太极) in the morning and dance in the evening.
Last week a reporter from a magazine asked her how she kept so young and healthy. She replied, "have a secret for staying young and healthy. It is quite simple. Keep your mind active，be interested in the world around you and learn at least one new thing every day, don’t think you are too old to learn. I know a man called Jack who started to study medicine in a college when he was 70 years old. He studied there for 6 years and now he works in a hospital. Another man called Barry began writing at the age of 71. and now he is a good writer. Some people may think keeping learning is easy only for young people.
In fact, everyone can make it.” It would be a good start to follow the old lady's example. I hope all the people will stay young and healthy.
