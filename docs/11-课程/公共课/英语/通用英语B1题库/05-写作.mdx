## Test Two

要求：请你以贝蒂的身份给你的好朋友琳达写一封关于去北京旅游的邮件，讲述你居住之地，所到之地以及旅游感受。
字数控制在80-100之间。

### 参考答案

Hi, Linda,

　　Last summer my parents took me to Beijing.
We stayed at Huabei Hotel. On the first day, we went to the Great Wall which is the longest wall in the world.
Lots of people from different countries like climbing the Great Wall. We’re very proud of it.

　　The following days, we went to the Summer Palace, Tian Tan, North Lake and Xiang Hill.
I now know more about the history of China. We ate delicious Beijing Duck.
I really enjoyed the trip to Beijing.
We’re tired but excited.
I’ll never forget the days in Beijing.
Hope to hear from you soon.


<div style={{textAlign: 'right'}}>Yours,<br />Betty</div>
